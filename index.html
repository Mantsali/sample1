Siyenzele Business Club

We are a National Business Club comprising of South African SMME’s who joined resources together for business advancement of our members.

The club constitutes of the board of directors which does project procurement analysis, raising funds, arranging workshops as financing members government business Projects. We are registered as business club under societies and club laws / act of South Africa with our Clubs account being held by Standard Bank of South Africa.

It is Our vision is to ensu re that our member’s projects don’t fall short or fails due to financing and or having proper business support as well as building business inter dependency within our members.
