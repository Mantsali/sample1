Terms of being member

By joining Siyenzel Business Club I understand that:

  • When the Club funds my projects, i will be charged a bit of interest on borrowed funds of the club. (This will time to time depending on risks on the project and be determined by the board). The board will publish fair interest rates per sector depending on project risks
  • I permit the board of the club to arrange cession to ensure that borrowed funds which is money belonging to the club is fully paid up and on time since its circulating funds to help our fellow members
  • I promise that my membership is per business. I do understand that business as a juristic person and will have own membership. I understand that each of my businesses will be treated as single member and each member will be registered / join on its own
  • I understand that I can’t sue the club if my project wasn’t funded due to funds unavailability. I understand the funds circulates within club members. (The board will do its best to help members to be funded be it by the club or using external financing houses like banks )
  • I understand that little interests changed when borrowing clubs funds for my projects will be used by the board for running office / including employees’ salaries since club’s membership funds are specifically directed to member’s business development. (Board will also invest part of the interests so as to increase members funds to avoid failing to fund members )
  • I understand that our Board of directors has powers to purchase other investment opportunities for us members from which proceeds derived will be at board discretion be capitalised to Members funds or paid to us members as annual bonus.
  • I further understand that the Board of directors will keep terms of membership updated so it’s my duty and responsibility to timeously keep myself updated by checking it too despite board’s efforts to keep me updated via my email address herein supplied.
  • I further understand that it’s my duty and responsibility to keep the office of the Secretary of the club Updated of any of changes of my details
